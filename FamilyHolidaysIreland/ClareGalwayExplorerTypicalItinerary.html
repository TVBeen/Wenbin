Clare & Galway Explorer - Typical Itinerary

7 Nights & 7 Rounds of Golf - from 985 Euro per person

Day 1 - Galway Bay Golf Club

Meet with Tailor-Made Golf Tour representative or chauffeur on arrival at Shannon Airport. Transfer to Oranmore and Galway Bay Golf & Country Club. Play Galway Bay, which its name suggests is situated on the southern shores of the famous bay. With magnificent views across to Galway City and west towards the north coast of Clare, the golf course presents a stern challenge. Wind is again the main factor, but there are several lakes, streams and bunkers to be negotiated. There are numerous sites of historical interest on the course. Overnight in nearby Galway City.

Day 2 - Ballinrobe Golf Club

North of Galway passing Lakes Corrib and Mask is Ireland's Lake District, a beautiful region dominated by these two lakes and Lough Carra. Ballinrobe golf course is set in the grounds of an old agricultural college and provides a fine parkland test where the greens are always superb. Return to Galway.

Day 3 - Connemara Golf Club

Journey through the heart of Connemara, a hauntingly bleak, but beautiful landscape. In the far southwestern corner of County Galway, Connemara golf links is a wonderfully natural links designed by Eddies Hackett. The inward nine in particular are memorable for the chellenging variety of holes. Return to Galway via Roundstone and the coast of Galway Bay. Overnight Galway Bay.

Day 4 - Gort Golf Club

Just south of Galway on the edge of the famour Burren region lies Gort Golf Club. Providing an interesting challenge in a scenic setting, Gort was designed by local native Christy O'Connor Junior. Continue to Ennis via the picturesque towns of Kinvara and Ballyvaughan and through the heart of the Burren. Overnight Ennis.

Day 5 - Woodstock Golf Club

Just outside the town of Ennis, Woodstock is a tranquil parklandcourse. Well defended by streams, bunkers and trees, Woodstock features a stretch of holes around a lake and an excellent finishing stretch. Overnight Ennis.

Day 6 - East Clare Golf Club

Another scenic challenge in a rural location, east of Ennis near the beautiful Lough Derg region. East Clare has four outstanding par-3's and also features challenging long holes that require accuracy of approach. Overnight in Ennis.

Day 7 - Lahinch Golf Club

A final day treat at one of the world's best courses. Lahinch is 20 miles west of Ennis and the golf course is adhacent to this popular seaside town. Lahinch combines a terrific links golf course with breathtaking ocean views and a wonderful golfing ambience in the clubhouse and town. The famous Cliffs of Moher are nearby and Doolin is a renowned Irish music centre. Final night in Ennis.

Day 8 - Departure from Shannon Airport

Transfer to airport for onward departure.
